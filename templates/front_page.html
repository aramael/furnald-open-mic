Furnald RHLO Presents

Open Mic Night

Details

Where?

Furnald Lounge in Furnald Hall

When?

Feburary 22nd 2013 starting at 9:00 PM

What?

You, and all of the other Columbia students have a chance to show all of us your skills. You can get up on stage and do absolutley anything. If you can burp the alphabet, sing better than Adele, form a band in record breaking time, tell jokes that can insult and cause laughs, practice some ventroliquism, then we need you to perform. The spirit of the open mike, of course, is to give anyone a chance to perform. If you don't think you have anything to share, just turn up and watch. It’s weird. Be okay with it being weird. You should totally come.

Why?

Because it's awesome and you can! Come around and support your friends and fellow Columbians.
